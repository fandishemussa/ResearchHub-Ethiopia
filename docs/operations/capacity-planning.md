# Capacity planning

Capacity is the highest repeatable workload meeting the selected SLOs on documented hardware. Record connected and active users separately. Increase one dimension at a time, observe saturation, and preserve 30% headroom for traffic variance and recovery.

Do not state that ResearchHub supports 1,000 users until a 1,000-connected-user test with at least 200 active users meets latency and error targets without pool exhaustion, unbounded memory, lost imports, duplicate jobs, or failed recovery.
